prithvi cafe

i had visited in december 2018

Quant little resturaunt tucked inside a by-lane of Jahu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. The resturaunt has a reseonably good menu at resoanable prices. The food is delicous, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by two people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of celebrities during show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointments. On other days, the crowd there is hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi Theater Fest", which you absoulutely MUST attend if possible.
